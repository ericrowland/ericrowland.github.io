Hi! I'm an Assistant Professor in the Department of Mathematics at Hofstra University.

I study arithmetic properties of integer sequences that arise in combinatorial settings. This involves a nice mix of number theory, combinatorics, and theoretical computer science.

I am currently working on several projects with my postdoc, Manon Stipulanti.

I co-organized sessions on Sequences, Words, and Automata at JMM 2020 and Arithmetic Properties of Sequences from Number Theory and Combinatorics at JMM 2017. In 2015 I organized a Workshop on Automatic Sequences in Liège, Belgium.
